West Campus

As Campus Pastor, I want to welcome you to the West Campus of The Austin Stone. “Welcomed” is what you will feel from the moment you walk in the doors of O. Henry Middle School. You’ll notice people lingering before and after the service in conversations. You’ll see out-of-breath parents delighted to drop off their carload of kids… because the KidStuff ministry is so good. You’ll see TeenRock digging in deep with your students in their own worship service. And you will walk into the gym to find a multi-generational congregation responding to Christ-centered worship and preaching.

Our vision is simply to see all of Austin, especially West Austin, worship Jesus. And if we stick a name tag on you, don’t be offended...it’s because we actually want to know you..

Your Campus Pastor,
Zac Allen
