Downtown Campus

Our Downtown Campus is located on 1715 W. Cesar Chavez at Austin High School. After outgrowing our initial space at Fulmore Middle School, The Austin Stone Community Church decided to move to Austin High School to accommodate for growth. This campus has four services, is our largest campus, and meets in the gymnasium of the high school.

West Campus

Our West Campus is located on 2610 W. 10th Street at O. Henry Middle School. Prior to becoming our West campus, Austin Bible Fellowship met here. Because of a similar vision for the renewal and restoration of the City of Austin, Austin Bible joined The Austin Stone to become our West Campus. This campus has one service and meets in the gymnasium of the middle school.

St. John Campus

Our St. John Campus is located on 500 E. St. Johns Avenue at The For the City Center and has four Sunday services. This campus was purposefully built to serve the St. John neighborhood. This permanent campus shares a space with The For the City Network where non-profit organizations work alongside the church for efforts of restoration and renewal of the area.

On Sundays

Sunday’s are a great time to gather together for worship, but if you're new, they can be daunting.
Here are a few hints to help navigate our Sunday morning services.

Follow The Signs

We've setup directional signage throughout our campuses to help you find your way. If you arrive early, follow the arrows to the Welcome Center – it's a good place to hang out until the service starts.

Welcome Team

Our welcome team members are walking knowledge banks who are there to help you find your way and answer questions. Look for a name tag, introduce yourself, and ask away.

Kidstuff

If you've got a child who is a 5th grade or under, follow the signs to the KidStuff check-in area. Our KidStuff staff would love to meet you and tell you about what we have on Sundays for your kids.

Coffee

We serve coffee each Sunday. Pick up a cup in the Welcome Center before the service.
