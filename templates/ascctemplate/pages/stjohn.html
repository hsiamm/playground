St. John Campus

Welcome to the St. John Campus of The Austin Stone Community Church! It is truly a blessing to meet at several campuses around the city. This allows us to retain a unified vision of exalting Jesus throughout Austin while meeting in smaller, more personalized, and diverse environments.

In the morning services you’ll worship alongside lots of young families, which keeps our KidStuff team happily busy. Both of our evening services are great places to hear and sing of the good news of Jesus, whether it's to end your Sunday or to begin a night of fellowship with your friends.

When you join us on Sunday, come eager and willing to meet some new friends because, while we’re big enough to sing loudly, we’re small enough to see the same faces each week.

Your Campus Pastors,
Jon Dansby and Chris Collins
