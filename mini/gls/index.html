Global Leadership Summit

Whether at church, school, business, or home, you are leading people and projects every day. You're leading in a culture that is constantly changing and redefining itself. Leadership takes perseverance, dedication, and a commitment to stretch your mind, expand your heart, and use your God-given gifts to impact your world in real and powerful ways.

On August 9-10, 2012, The For the City Network – in partnership with The Austin Stone Community Church – invites you to gather at The For the City Center for The Global Leadership Summit via satellite simulcast. Thousands of leaders from a variety of contexts – pastors, teachers, executives, managers, students, nonprofit leaders, etc. – will gather across North America and leverage the high caliber leadership content of The Global Leadership Summit to maximize their impact. Register now to be a part of this annual event designed to stretch and challenge you with world-class speakers, interactive dialogue, and practical training.

Check out this year’s speaker lineup, including Condoleezza Rice, Jim Collins, Bill Hybels, Geoffrey Canada, and Patrick Lencioni, among many others.
